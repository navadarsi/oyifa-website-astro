# Company Overview
Oyifa (اويفا) is the leading IT procurement and outsourcing platform for the GCC region. The name is inspired by the Arabic word "Ifa'a" (إيفاء), meaning fulfillment or completion. We connect businesses with qualified IT service providers across the Middle East, streamlining the tender and proposal process.

## What We Do
- IT Tender Management Platform
- Vendor-Client Matching for IT Services
- Proposal and RFP Management
- Regional IT Outsourcing Solutions across GCC countries

## Our Mission
To fix the broken IT procurement process in the GCC by eliminating endless sales calls, irrelevant pitches, and pressure tactics. We provide a transparent, honest, and simple platform for businesses to find and engage with qualified IT vendors.

## Key Features
- Rigorous vendor vetting process
- Priority for regional/local talent
- Transparent pricing and no hidden fees
- Multi-language support (English/Arabic)
- Bilingual platform with full RTL support

## Geographic Focus
Primary Markets: United Arab Emirates (UAE), Saudi Arabia (KSA), Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain, Oman

## Pricing Structure
We offer tiered subscription plans for vendors:

### For BHD, KWD, OMR:
- Free: Browse tenders, basic profile
- Basic: 49/month (499/year) - 10 proposals/month
- Standard: 99/month (999/year) - 50 proposals/month (Most Popular)
- Premium: 149/month (1,499/year) - Unlimited proposals

### For SAR, AED, QAR:
- Free: Browse tenders, basic profile
- Basic: 499/month (4,999/year) - 10 proposals/month
- Standard: 999/month (9,999/year) - 50 proposals/month (Most Popular)
- Premium: 1,499/month (14,999/year) - Unlimited proposals
